About Us

Welcome to Studio Temani, where moments become timeless memories through the art of photography. Founded in 2021 and located in the heart of Grand Galaxy, Bekasi, Studio Temani is dedicated to providing personalized photography services tailored to the needs of individuals, groups, families, and graduates. In the digital age, communicating information effectively about our photography services is a challenge we embrace. Studio Temani understands the importance of reaching our audience in a rapidly evolving online landscape, and we continually strive to enhance our online presence to provide information about our diverse photography offerings.

Studio

At Studio Temani, we create a warm and friendly environment where every guest is invited to experience the magic of photography. With a comfortable studio space and state-of-the-art photography equipment, we are committed to delivering an unforgettable photography experience.

So, in Studio Temani we have 2 Studio Facilities :

  • Modern Photography Studio: Equipped with cutting-edge photography equipment and professional lighting, our studio provides the perfect atmosphere to capture beautiful images.
  • Comfortable Waiting Area: Before or after the photo session, our guests can relax in a comfortable waiting area, complete with cooling facilities and light entertainment.
